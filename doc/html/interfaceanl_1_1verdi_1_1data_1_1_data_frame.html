DataFrame anl.verdi.data.DataFrame.slice ( Slice  slice) throws InvalidRangeException

Creates a new DataFrame that is a slice or subsection of this one. The two DataFrames will share data, so any change to one will be reflected in the other. The slice ranges should be in terms of the array indices of the data frame which are NOT necessarily the axes origins and extents.

Parameters
slicedescribes the slice of data contained by the new DataFrame.
Returns
a new DataFrame that is a slice or subsection of this one
Exceptions

DataFrame anl.verdi.data.DataFrame.sliceCopy ( Slice  slice) throws InvalidRangeException

Creates a new DataFrame that is a slice or subsection of this one. The two DataFrames will NOT share data, so any change to one will NOT be reflected in the other. The slice ranges should be in terms of the array indices of the data frame which are NOT necessarily the axes origins and extents.
